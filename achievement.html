ACHIEVEMENTS


SAILENT FEATURES

  • GNDU has a global recognition with alumni associated with top notch companies around the world.
  • With a strong legacy of over 50 years, GNDU has been established under the Punjab State Legislative Act No.21 of 1969.
  • Research funding from DST, UGC and other funding agencies worth crores of rupees.
  • World class Research-Oriented Infrastructure comprising of sophisticated instruments and softwares.
  • Access to latest research softwares such as Virtual Nano Lab, Optisystem, NetSim, MATLAB etc.
  • 9 Times National Cultural Champion, 13 times North Zone Inter-University Cultural Championship and 22 times winner of Maulana Abdul Kalam Azad Trophy.
  • Highly transparent evaluation system based on CGPA system.
  • Excellent placement record for both ECE & CSE streams.
